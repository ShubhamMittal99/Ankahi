The Dramatics Society of IIT Delhi



BRCA stands for the Board For Recreational and Creative Activities. It consists of ten clubs that conduct events - competitive and non- competitive throughout the year in IIT Delhi. BRCA also conducts Rendezvous each year, which is one of the largest college cultural festivals in India.
